Research

I'm a PhD student at Uppsala University. My research project covers modeling and Bayesian inference of complex and stochastic models, mainly epidemiological models, in the setting of intractable likelihoods (see approximate bayesian computations)

I've contributed to the following papers:

  • SimInf: An R package for data-driven stochastic disease spread simulations (2019, Published)
  • Bayesian epidemiological modeling over high-resolution network data (2019, Revision)
  • Towards confident Bayesian parameter estimation in stochastic chemical kinetics (2020, accepted)
